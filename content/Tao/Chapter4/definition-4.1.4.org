*Definition 4.1.4 (Negation of integers)*. /If $(a — b)$ is an integer, we define the negation $−(a — b)$ to be the integer $(b — a)$. In particular if $n = n — 0$ is a positive natural number, we can define its negation $−n = 0 —n$./
