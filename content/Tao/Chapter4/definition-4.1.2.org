*Definition 4.1.2* The sum of two integers, $(a — b) + (c —d)$, is defined by the formula $(a — b) + (c —d) := (a + c)— (b + d)$. The product of two integers, $(a — b) × (c — d)$, is defined by $(a —b) × (c — d) := (ac + bd)— (ad + bc)$.
